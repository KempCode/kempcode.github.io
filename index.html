About Me

Hello! I am a recent graduate from Massey University with a Bachelor of Science, majoring in Computer Science. I am passionate about software development, with a focus on full-stack development, Android app development, and security. I've had the privilege of working as a Freelance Web Developer and Technical Consultant, where I've undertaken diverse projects and responsibilities. Notably, I built the Mt Eden Community Association Limited website from scratch, assuming sole responsibility for all technology aspects of the project.

Utilizing Agile Scrum Methodology, I led the development process, creating a custom WordPress child theme with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. My responsibilities ranged from setting up hosting, VPS, web server, and emails to ensuring site security compliance and optimizing SEO. I'm proud to have delivered a seamless user experience while meeting stringent customer data compliance standards.

In addition to my freelance work, my academic journey at Massey University has equipped me with a breadth of technical skills and knowledge across various subjects, including Machine Learning, Networking, Artificial Intelligence, Security, Web Development, Database Development, and more. I have hands-on experience with C++, Python, HTML5, CSS3, Bootstrap, JavaScript, jQuery, and other technologies.

Throughout university, I completed numerous projects, including CLI programs and GUI applications using C++, such as a Covid Passport CRUD app and a Winsock CLI RSA CBC client/server application showcased on my portfolio website. In Python, I contributed to projects like a playable pyGame during a hackathon and a full-stack application for a small fictional Airport using Django and SQLite3.

My expertise extends to front-end development, where I've designed interfaces for numerous websites using HTML5, CSS3, Bootstrap, and JavaScript. I'm also proficient in Java, Android Studio, Oracle SQL, C# with ASP.NET, SQlite3, Haskell, Docker, Junit, Figma, GNS3, Kali Linux, GitHub, Jira, WordPress, and more.

I am currently available for hire and excited about the opportunity to contribute to impactful projects and bring my skills to your team.
